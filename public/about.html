About Sanjeevani

Sanjeevani was born from the belief that every life matters. We connect people across India to make
organ donation a reality bringing second chances to those in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the silent bridge between life and loss to ensure that no heartbeat fades in vain. We strive to inspire selfless giving, support grieving families, and create a world where every willing donor becomes a second chance for someone waiting in hope.

Our Vision

We envision a future where no one loses a loved one just because an organ wasn’t available in time a future where kindness moves faster than illness, where every person has access to life-saving transplants, and knows the power they hold to save a life.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We believe in empathy and support for every donor and recipient family.
  • Transparency: We ensure open, honest communication and ethical practices.
  • Commitment: We are dedicated to saving lives and creating long-lasting change in organ donation awareness.
