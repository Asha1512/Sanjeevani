Organ Donation Awareness

Knowledge is the first step to saving lives. Learn the facts about organ donation and discover how you
can be part of this incredible journey of giving hope.

Myths vs Facts

Age
Myth

I’m too old to be an organ donor

Fact

There’s no age limit for donation. People in their 80s have donated organs. Medical suitability is decided at time of death.

Funeral
Myth

Organ donation will affect my funeral or appearance

Fact

The process is respectful and allows for open-casket funerals. Donation doesn’t disfigure the body.

Medical Conditions
Myth

I can't donate because I have a medical condition

Fact

Few conditions automatically disqualify you. Each donor is medically evaluated at the time of death to decide suitability.

Religion
Myth

My religion doesn’t allow organ donation

Fact

All major religions support organ donation as a final act of love, compassion, and saving lives.
